    HELLO!

    I'M CHRIS GOODMAN. A FREELANCE WEB DENIGNER

    You are probably sat wondering who this Chris guy is?

    Im a freelance web developer based in Taunton, Somerset. I work with businesses both local and UK wide; ranging from freelancers, startups, small and medium enterprises to large companies. I provide my clients a solution that puts their company and or personal brands into the eyes of their consumers.

    To make things easier, In addition to FREE advice, I offer a number of services including web hosting, email and search engine business listing.

    WEB DESIGN

    In this day and age every company should have an online presence, big or small you need to let the world know about your business. I can create you a unique website designed around your current business to let the world know who you are and what you do!

    WEB DEVELOPMENT

    Once your site is designed I will develop it into an interactive site with any features you require to get the most out of your site. Be this a content management driven site you can update yourself or a complete online store - I have the skills to create this for you and your business.

    WEBHOSTING

    If you want it, I can offer you a fast shared server to host your shiny new website. can include an email address that reflects your domain name. Speaking of domain name, if you would like me to register that and point it at the server, I can do that too! It's all in the service.

    GOOGLE BUSINESS LISTING AND SEO

    Any good freelance web designer knows that there is no magic formular to get your website to the top of popular search engines. So I ensure that your title tags, heading tags and keywords are all set up, to give your website the very best chance to get to that coveted position. I will register your business on Google so that you apear right there when your prospective customers search for you.

    To The Future

    I don't just leave it there! Once your website is live I systematically monitor it to ensure your server and all its components are up to date, so you don't have to worry about the technical bits. Website support comes as part of the package, so if you need advice I'm always here to help...
